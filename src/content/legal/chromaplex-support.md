## Frequently Asked Questions

### The game crashes on launch

Make sure you are running iOS 17.0 or later. Try deleting and reinstalling the App.

### My progress was lost

Game progress is stored locally on your device. If you deleted and reinstalled the App, progress cannot be recovered. A future update may include iCloud sync.

### How do daily challenges work?

A new puzzle is available every day. Complete it to maintain your streak. Streaks are tracked on the Game Center leaderboard.

### How do I report a bug?

Please open a GitHub issue with:

1. Your device model and iOS version
2. A description of what happened
3. Steps to reproduce the issue

## Data Deletion

Chromaplex stores all game data locally on your device. To delete all data associated with the App:

1. Open **Settings** on your iOS device
2. Go to **General** > **iPhone Storage**
3. Find and tap **Chromaplex**
4. Tap **Delete App**

This removes all locally stored game data including progress, streaks, and settings. No data is stored on external servers, so no additional deletion request is needed.

If you have used Game Center features, you can manage that data through your Apple ID settings.

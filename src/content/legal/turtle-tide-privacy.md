## Overview

Turtle Tide ("the App") is developed by Jason Chalky. This privacy policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects your information.

## Information We Collect

### Game Data

All game progress — level stars, turtle collection XP, daily challenge streaks, currency balances, settings — is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

### GameKit / Game Center

If you use Game Center features (leaderboards, achievements), Apple handles that data according to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/). The App submits anonymous scores for total stars, daily challenge results, and timed rush scores. Game Center sign-in is optional — the App functions fully without it.

### In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit 2. The App receives confirmation of verified purchases but does not collect, store, or transmit payment information. Apple's [standard privacy terms](https://www.apple.com/legal/internet-services/itunes/) apply.

### What We Do NOT Collect

- No user accounts or personal identifiers
- No advertising identifier (IDFA) — the App does not show ads
- No analytics or telemetry
- No location data
- No camera, microphone, or photo library access
- No contacts or calendar access
- No server calls of any kind

## Third-Party Services

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| Apple GameKit | Leaderboards & Achievements (optional) | [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/) |
| Apple StoreKit 2 | In-App Purchases | [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/) |

## Data Retention

Local game data persists until you delete the App. We do not retain any user data on external servers.

## Children's Privacy

Turtle Tide is suitable for players of all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

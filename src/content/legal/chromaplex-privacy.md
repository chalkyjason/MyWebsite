## Overview

Chromaplex ("the App") is developed by Jason Chalky. This privacy policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects your information.

## Information We Collect

### Advertising Data

The App uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements, including rewarded ads. AdMob may collect:

- Device advertising identifier (IDFA)
- General location data (IP-based, not precise)
- Device information (model, OS version)
- Ad interaction data

The App uses Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework to request your consent before tracking. You can change your tracking preference at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

### Consent Management

The App uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to manage advertising consent in compliance with GDPR and other regional privacy regulations. You may be presented with a consent dialog on first launch depending on your region.

### Ad Attribution

The App participates in SKAdNetwork for anonymous ad attribution. This does not collect personal information.

### Game Data

Game progress, scores, level completion, daily challenge streaks, and settings are stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to any server.

### GameKit / Game Center

If you use Game Center features (leaderboards, achievements), Apple handles that data according to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/). The App submits anonymous scores for total stars, longest streak, and levels completed.

### Push Notifications

The App may request permission to send push notifications for daily challenge reminders. This is optional and can be disabled in your device settings.

## Data Retention

Local game data persists until you delete the App. We do not retain any user data on external servers.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. AdMob is configured to comply with COPPA requirements.

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

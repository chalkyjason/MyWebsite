Turtle Tide is a match-3 puzzle game that rewards you for caring about its characters, not just clearing its board. Every match feeds the evolution of the species you matched — hatchlings grow into juveniles, adults, elders, and legendaries. It's the "one more game" hook the genre has been missing, wrapped in hand-drawn chibi illustrations that never look AI-generic.

### Why This Game

Most match-3 games ask you to match tiles. Turtle Tide asks you to care *which* tiles you match. Every matching turtle species earns XP that evolves your collection, unlocking abilities that change how the next match plays. Combined with ocean current physics, auto-fire power-ups that land the "wow" moment instantly instead of making you chain twice, and 100 hand-tuned levels, it's match-3 with the retention loop of a roguelike.

### How It Works

Swipe to swap adjacent creatures. Match 3 of a kind to clear them. Match 4 instantly fires a rocket across the full row or column — no saving specials for later. Match 5 sweeps every tile of that color off the board in a chromatic wave. An L or T shape drops a 3×3 bomb and leaves a glowing Violet Turtle at the intersection — swap it with any neighbor to fire a plus-shape wipe that takes out an entire row AND column at once. Match 6 or more triggers a 5×5 mega-blast with a screen flash.

Ocean currents determine how tiles fall: downward in the shallows, sideways in Open Sea, toward whirlpool centers in Ancient Reef, upward in the Deep Trench, and shifting every single turn in Arctic Waters. Early levels use a smaller tile pool so matches feel dense and satisfying; later worlds widen the roster and tighten the challenge. Every 20 levels a boss appears with its own attack pattern and HP phases.

### Privacy-First by Design

Turtle Tide stores all game data on-device. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no third-party servers. Optional Game Center leaderboards use anonymous identifiers managed by Apple. In-app purchases go straight through StoreKit 2 — no personal data collected. You can play the entire campaign with airplane mode on.

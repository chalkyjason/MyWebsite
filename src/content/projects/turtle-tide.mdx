Turtle Tide is a match-3 puzzle game that rewards you for caring about its characters, not just clearing its board. Match turtle tiles to feed their evolution — hatchlings grow into juveniles, adults, elders, and legendaries. It's the "one more game" hook the genre has been missing.

### Why This Game

Most match-3 games ask you to match tiles. Turtle Tide asks you to care *which* tiles you match. Every matching turtle species earns XP that evolves your collection, unlocking abilities that change how the next match plays. Combined with ocean current physics, boss battles, and 100 hand-tuned levels, it's match-3 with the retention hooks of a roguelike.

### How It Works

Swipe to swap adjacent tiles. Match 3+ of a kind to clear them. Match 4+ to earn special tiles. Combine two specials for mega effects — Starfish + Jellyfish clears a row plus all tiles of one species. Ocean currents determine how tiles fall: downward in the shallows, sideways in open water, toward whirlpool centers in the reef, upward in the trench, and shifting every turn in the arctic.

### Privacy-First by Design

Turtle Tide stores all game data on-device. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking. Optional Game Center leaderboards use anonymous identifiers managed by Apple. In-app purchases are unlocked directly — no server calls, no personal data collected.

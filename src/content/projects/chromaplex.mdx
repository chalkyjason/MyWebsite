Chromaplex is a modern take on the classic Mastermind code-breaking game. Guess the secret color code using logical deduction — each guess gives you feedback via black and white pegs to narrow down the solution.

### How It Works

Each level presents a hidden color code. You place colored pegs in a row and submit your guess. Black pegs mean right color, right position. White pegs mean right color, wrong position. Use logic to crack the code within the allowed attempts.

### Difficulty Tiers

Progress through six difficulty levels, each increasing the challenge:

- **Tutorial** — 3 colors, 4 options, 10 attempts
- **Beginner** through **Master** — up to 5 colors, 8 options, and only 6 attempts

### Beyond Mastermind

Chromaplex includes additional puzzle modes — Ball Sort, Binary Grid, and Flow Connect — giving you variety when you want a break from code-breaking.

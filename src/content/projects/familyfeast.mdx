FamilyFeast solves "What's for dinner?" by turning meal planning into a collaborative family activity. Instead of one person deciding for everyone, the whole family votes -- and a consensus algorithm picks meals that actually make everyone happy.

### Why this app

Meal planning is stressful when one person carries the whole load. FamilyFeast distributes the decision across the family using ranked-choice voting. The Borda Count algorithm avoids polarizing picks -- it doesn't just find the most-loved option, it finds the option with the broadest support. Nobody gets stuck eating something they hate.

### How it works

Add recipes manually or browse suggestions. Create a voting session and each family member swipes through the candidates -- Like, Dislike, OK, Super Like, or Veto (for allergies and hard constraints). The algorithm scores everything and surfaces the top picks. Schedule winners to specific days, and the app generates a shopping list organized by aisle with cost estimates.

### Privacy-first by design

No account creation. No tracking. No analytics. No ads. All data lives on-device via SwiftData with optional CloudKit sync using Apple's end-to-end encryption. The only identifier is your anonymous iCloud User ID for multi-device sync -- we never see your name, email, or Apple ID.
